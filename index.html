PRIVACY STATEMENT

The Privacy Act 1998 regulates the collection and use, disclosure and maintenance of personal information collected by Real Estate Agents from people attending Open for Inspections.

It is a condition of entry that before entering this property you must give your name and current telephone number to the TP Dynamics representative. In addition you also may give your email address.

This information is collected for the following reasons:

  1. Security reasons
  2. To allow us to call or email you after the open for inspection to establish your interest in the property and to inform you of other properties we have listed for sale that may suit your needs.
  3. To send you information from time to time regarding the local property market.
  4. To enable us to give feedback to the vendor regarding interest in the property. In this case only your surname or given name will be disclosed.
  5. The information is used for internal purposes only and phone numbers and email addresses will NOT be disclosed to any other third party (except as in 3 above) without your written consent.
  6. If you do not wish to be called as part of our normal follow up procedures please advise the Sales Consultant before leaving.

All personal information we collect is held electronically and in hard copy in our office in a secure manner. It will only be used for the purposes noted above and will not be disclosed to any third party or recipients in other countries without your written permission, unless required by law. However, we are not liable for any unauthorised access of this information.

If you do not wish to be contacted as part of our normal follow up procedures please advise the Sales Consultant before leaving.

You have the right to view the information collected about you, the right to correct the information if it is not accurate, up to date or complete. Should you ask us to remove your information from our records we will do so securely.

If you consider we have breached the Australian Privacy Principles you may contact us on 02 6154 8900 or email: admin@tpdynamics.com.au. We will promptly consider your complaint and attempt to resolve it in a timely manner. If we are unable to resolve it you may refer your complaint to the office of the Australian Information Commissioner, GPO Box 5218, Sydney 2001 or enquiries@oaic.govau.

DESIGN

Imagine the best that Canberra’s Inner North offers, wrap that in mature eucalypt forests and nestle it at the foot of a mountain … and call it Aranda. Named after the Arrente people of central Australia, Aranda is minutes from the CBD, close to universities and shopping precincts and moments away from myriad recreational escapes.

Sparks is its own world within leafy Aranda, soaking up the established culture, environment and history but emerging fresh with its thoughtful architecture, energy efficiency and sustainable approach.

The Aranda Primary School motto urges us to ‘Grasp the things that are good’. Sparks residences seize the good life in the beautiful Bush Capital.

SPACE

Sparks residences offer all the convenience, privacy and energy efficiency that Canberrans should expect from sustainable boutique development. Respecting the existing character of the neighbourhood, the development is split into two separate built forms comprising three three-bedroom residences, creating six dwellings overall. The considered design provides articulation within sustainable medium density urban living. Overall accommodation is substantial, with 3 bedrooms and separate living spaces as well as generous garden spaces creating individual oases within the Sparks community.

THE VISION

Inspired by the examples of classic mid-century modern architecture preserved on Aranda’s leafy streets, Sparks interiors are a delight to be in. Bright white lexicon quarter walls create the perfect gallery for your art collection. The kitchen continues the curated vibe with warm timber finishes such as blackbutt flooring, stone benchwork and decorative tiling, recipe book and display shelves and a fresh colour palette. The quality continues through to the bathroom with elegant tiling and brushed nickel finishes providing beauty and wearability. Atrium inspired courtyards create privacy and beauty in outlook from both kitchen and bathroom.

SOLAR

Owners of Sparks residences can feel proud of the environmental integrity of their home and their contribution to Canberra’s world-class renewable energy standards. With each residence completed with a 5 kW solar system you’ll save on energy bills while reducing your carbon footprint. Consideration of solar access continues through the architectural design with living spaces oriented for clever seasonal sun penetration.

INCLUSIONS

Built to last, quality inclusions throughout mean that you’ll never have to think of upgrading in the future. Each residence is fitted out with high specification Bosch appliances in the kitchen, so you can experience quality and reliability every day, from creating your culinary masterpieces, to keeping your glasses sparkling. Aesthetically pleasing and energy efficient, you’ll also be saving water and electricity on a daily basis. Each residence is outfitted with stain resistant and beautiful ‘Lobelia’ Triexta carpet, and waterproof floorboards for a fuss free day to day. The list of convenient inclusions includes comprehensive inbuilt joinery to take care of storage; double glazed windows, contributing to energy efficiency alongside ducted gas heating and reverse cycle air conditioning for comfort.
